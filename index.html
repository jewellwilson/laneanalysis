Lane Analysis Explained
All values are aggregated based upon the calculated rate formula and engine settings included in the analysis.
  • Line Haul Rate:
    		• Calculated rate not including fuel surcharges or accessorials.
  • Fuel Cost:
    		• Fuel surcharges that were included when rates were captured. For market rates that do not include a fuel surcharge, Truckstop.com will use a default $0.05 fuel scale.
  • All-In Rate:
    		• Calculated rate including fuel surcharges and/or accessorials.
